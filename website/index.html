What is sdns://2021?

sdns://2021 is a fun and informal online event focused on real-world DNS privacy technologies, and an opportunity to celebrate 10 years of DNS privacy improvements.

When will it be held?

The event will be held on August 14th, 2021, from 5pm UTC.
It is totally free, but you will need to register, so we know how many people are interested in the event and plan accordingly.

Who is this for?

This event is for anyone who is interested in practical DNS privacy, or who wants to learn more about how it works.
It is open to anyone, regardless of skill level.

Format

We will have short (~15 minute + QA) sessions on a variety of topics, followed by a round table on the future of DNS privacy.

Sample topics

  • DNS-based censorship in China
  • Operating encrypted DNS resolvers at scale
  • Anonymized DNSCrypt and Oblivious DoH
  • Maintaining public blocklists
  • Securing encrypted DNS resolvers
  • Encrypted Client Headers (ESNI)
  • Encrypted DNS, Tor and VPNs
  • Research/experiments
  • Alternative DNS roots
  • Blockchain-based DNS
  • Interesting new DNS tools and applications
The program will be posted here in the forthcoming days.

How can I help?

Want to help out with the event? We are looking for speakers, helpers, and helpers of helpers.
Presentations must be short. A lightning talk can be 5 minute or less. A presentation should ideally not exceed 15 minute but this is not a hard limit.
It can be live or pre-recorded, if you feel more comfortable with that.
This is an open and informal event, not a conference.
Anything that could be useful to other people is welcome, as long as it remains somehow related to improving privacy.
